# 🧗 Faster Ladder Climbing

**No Backports:** This mod targets **Minecraft 26.1+**. Older versions are unsupported.

> **Ascend with Purpose. No more vertical waiting games.**

Tired of the slow, monotonous crawl up your mega-base's towers? **Faster Ladder Climbing** overhauls vertical traversal, allowing you to scale ladders and vines at blazing speeds without the janky "physics launch" common in other mods. Engineered with **Zenith Protocol v2.1** standards, it ensures a lightweight, stable, and highly configurable experience.

Part of the **Instant Gratification Collection** — mods designed to remove friction and keep you in the flow of the game.

---

## ✨ Features

### ⚡ Blazing Vertical Ascent
Scale any climbable surface (Ladders, Vines, Scaffolding) with a configurable speed multiplier. Move from the bedrock to the sky in a fraction of the vanilla time.

### 🛡️ Momentum Safety (Peak Clamp)
We've solved the "Sky Launching" problem. When you reach the top of a ladder, the mod intelligently dampens your vertical velocity. This ensures you land firmly on the edge rather than being shot into the air like a rocket.

### ⚙️ Entity Agnostic Scaling
It's not just for players! All `LivingEntity` instances (mobs, villagers, animals) respect the climbing speed multiplier, allowing for faster mob farms or more dangerous vertical mob encounters.

### 🌐 Server-Side Synchronization
Built with multiplayer stability in mind. The mod is primarily server-side, ensuring all players experience consistent, lag-free vertical movement without requiring complex client-side configurations.

## 🛠️ Configuration (Native Game Rules)

No clunky configuration files or complex menus. Faster Ladder Climbing utilizes the **Native Minecraft Game Rules** system for real-time adjustments.

| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| `ig_climbing_speed_multiplier` | `100` | `300` | Scales climbing speed (0% to 500%). |
